$$\boxed{E_{r}=\frac{2kP \cos{\theta}}{r^{3}}}$$
$$\boxed{E_{\theta}=\frac{kP \sin{\theta}}{r^{3}}}$$
$$\boxed{E_{net}=\sqrt{E_{r}^{2}+E_{\theta}^{2}}}$$
$$\boxed{E_{net}=\frac{kP}{r^{3}}\sqrt{1+3 \cos^2{\theta}}}$$
Direction:
$\alpha=\tan^{-1}({\frac{E_{\theta}}{E_{r}}})$
