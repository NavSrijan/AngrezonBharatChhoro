title:Gauss's Law 
It states that, "_Total [[Electric Flux]] linked to a closed surface is equal to _$\frac{1}{\in_{0}}$ _times the charge inside the close surface._"

$$\boxed{\phi_{net}=\frac{q_{in}}{\in_{0}}}$$
